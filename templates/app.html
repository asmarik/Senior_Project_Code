Available Analysis Methods

Choose from our two powerful compliance analysis methods to assess your policy documents

AI-Powered

Gap and Match Analysis

The Gap and Match Analysis module provides an article-level breakdown of all regulatory requirements. It automatically identifies which articles are fully covered, partially covered, or missing, allowing users to see exactly where compliance issues exist.

Detects missing or weak areas in uploaded documents
Classifies results into Full, Partial, or Missing
Highlights reasons for missing articles with automated explanations
Provides a clean, structured view to quickly understand compliance gaps
AI-Powered

Recommendation Analysis

The Recommendation Analysis provides smart, context-aware actions to help improve compliance. It analyzes the detected gaps and suggests specific remediation steps, best practices, and targeted fixes.

Generates tailored improvement recommendations based on missing or partially covered articles
Prioritizes fixes by impact and urgency
Suggests updates, revisions, or additions to meet legal requirements
Uses hybrid retrieval + LLM reasoning for accurate, context-aligned suggestions
